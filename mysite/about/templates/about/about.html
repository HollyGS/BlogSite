Hi, I'm Holly...


I am a recent engineering grad with plans to soul search on a gap year before entering 50 odd years of work. One month into my adventures I found myself quarantined in a hostel in South America, unsure when travel restrictions might be lifted. I returned to my family home and with the suggestion to write a blog, a wave of enthusiasm washed over me... and that brings us to the present day. I learned to develop a website and now I'm writing an about me page.
I will be reflecting on life events, aiming to gain some clarity in the chaos of the world. I intend to be open and honest when sharing my perspectives. My hope is that this hobby might spark some discussion and that we will learn something new. Here's to discovery!
